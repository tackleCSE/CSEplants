# CSEplants
Drag and match game for plants in California Supplemental Exam (CSE) for landscape architects

Southern California plants come from The Drought Tolerant Garden - Los Angeles County Handbook
https://dpw.lacounty.gov/wwd/web/Documents/DroughtTolerantGarden.pdf

Northern California plants come from THRIFTY 150 - Durable, Low Maintenance, Water Wise Plants Recommended by San Francisco Public Works
https://sfpublicworks.org/sites/default/files/THRIFTY%20ONE-FIFTY_FINAL_10_22_15.pdf

When selecting the images for each plant, I tried to have the upper image to show the overall size and context, and the lower image to show details of leaves/flowers/fruits etc.
I recommend play the drag & match game a few rounds first and memorize the water use, exposure, native species etc. in the pdf later.
